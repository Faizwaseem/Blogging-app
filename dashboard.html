What is the concept of the iPhone 15? The standard iPhone 15 model
inherits several features from last year's iPhone 14 Pro series, including
an improved Dynamic Island, the A16 Bionic chip, and a 48MP main camera.
There are additionally five colours to choose from in a textured matte finish:
pink, yellow, blue, green and black.
